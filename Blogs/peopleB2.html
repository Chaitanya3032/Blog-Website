10 Quotes to Motivate Yourself Every Day

SARKAR DWIVEDI BLOG
07 January 2021 | 6 min read

10 Quotes to Motivate Yourself Every Day – If you are always worried about your success, and keep thinking about your future, then these 10 ideas will help you in getting success in your life, so let’s start.


“I was thinking one day and I realized that if I just had somebody behind me all the way to motivate me I could make a big difference. Nobody came along like that so I just became that person for myself.” -Unknown


“People often say that motivation doesn’t last. Well, neither does bathing -that’s why we recommend it daily”. – Zig Ziglar


“Don’t Let Yesterday Take Up Too Much Of Today”. – Will Rogers


“Failure Will Never Overtake Me If My Determination To Succeed Is Strong Enough.”- Og Mandino


“We Generate Fears While We Sit. We Overcome Them By Action”. – Dr. Henry Link


“Reading Is To The Mind, As Exercise Is To The Body.” – Brian Tracy


“Working hard for something we don’t care about is called stressed; working hard for something we love is called passion.” -Simon Sinek


“Action is the foundational key to all success.” -Pablo Picasso


“Done is better than perfect.” – Sheryl Sandberg


“The act of doing something un-does the fear.” -Shonda Rhimes
